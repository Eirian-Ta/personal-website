About me

I love studying languages, including programming languages. Because every time I learn a new language, I see myself learning a new way of thinking and looking at the world. Coding is an excellent way of communicating. If the computer does not understand what you want it to do, you have to ask yourself what you have done wrong and try different ways to fix it. You do not give up until the computer gets it. I think that is the point. You have to review yourself before blaming others. It might take a lot of failures to achieve success. But you also learn from your failures. The important thing is 'never give up'. Coding taught me all that.

coding == meditating

A friend asked me: "What do you do in your free time?" And I said: "I code."

I do coding because I love it, just as simple as that.
